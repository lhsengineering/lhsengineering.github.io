About

Our club holds weekly meetings during Monday lunches in Ms. Frazita’s room (409). We talk about all of the branches of engineering, compete in competitions, and have technical demos.

Our competitions are team-based and revolve around various engineering topics. Along with TEAMS, our oldest and most popular competition, there are several more opportunities to win national and international awards.
